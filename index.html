Features

Everything your team needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Built for captains and players who want to spend less time in group chats and more time on the pitch.

Lineups

Captains build and publish match lineups with a tap. Players get notified instantly so everyone knows who's starting.

Availability

Players mark themselves available or unavailable for each match. Captains see the full picture before building lineups.

Match Schedule

View your full season schedule with upcoming and past matches, scores, and results all in one place.

Team Management

Invite players via link, assign roles, and transfer captaincy when the time comes. Your roster, your rules.

Push Notifications

Never miss a lineup announcement, availability reminder, or match update. Stay in the loop without opening the app.
