Delete Your Account

All Hands Sports

You can permanently delete your All Hands account and all associated data directly from within the app. This action is immediate and cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (name, email, avatar)
  • Your team membership
  • Your availability and lineup data
  • All notifications associated with your account

Note: If you are a team captain, you must transfer captaincy to another player before deleting your account.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the All Hands app
  2. Tap Profile in the bottom navigation
  3. Tap the settings icon in the top right
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Need help?

If you are unable to delete your account from within the app, contact us at support@playallhands.com and we will delete your account and data manually within 30 days.
